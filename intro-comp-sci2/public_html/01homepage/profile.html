A Random Rant About The Word Orange

Why did we make the word orange? That word is so confusing! Why is it both a fruit and a color? Did someone just look at a color, and decide to name a fruit based off of it? Who thinks that our collection of colors is a repository to go looking for a name for a fruit? What do those things have to do with one another?
Why does this persist in other languages too? Shouldn't there be a point where one person looks at the fruit, and goes: "this is stupid, why do we keep on doing this? Just because an orange is orange, doesn't mean we should call it that." How could we as a society screw this up that badly? I believe that this is enough reason to call a state of emergency in order to solve this crisis before it becomes a lost cause.
